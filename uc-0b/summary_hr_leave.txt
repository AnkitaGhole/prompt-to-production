CMC EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY — KEY OBLIGATIONS SUMMARY
=====================================================
Clause 2.3: Application must be submitted at least 14 days in advance using Form HR-L1.
Clause 2.4: Written approval from direct manager is required before leave commences; verbal approval is not valid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence is recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
Clause 2.6: Maximum annual leave carry-forward is 5 days; excess days are forfeited on 31 December.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) or they are forfeited.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of return.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave immediately before/after holidays or annual leave requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from both the Department Head and the HR Director; manager approval alone is insufficient.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
